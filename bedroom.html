Did you know that plants could improve your sleep?

It is known that plants could have a calming effect that could help your bedroom's environment, but their benefits don't stop there. There are many different ways a plant could help you wake up refreshed. Here you will find which are the best plants!

Best air-purifying plants
  1. Aloe Vera: known for its skin benefits, it also removes benzene and formaldehyde from the air.
  2. Snake plant: also knwon as the Sansevieria plant. It tolerates low (bedroom) light and thrives off some neglect. Did you know that snake plants even convert carbon dioxide to oxygen during the night? This is proven by several studies and thus makes this plant the green star in your bedroom!
  3. Lavender: besides it relaxing smell, lavender has been proven to lower heart rate, blood pressure and stress levels, which is why it’s perfect for the bedroom. It’s one of the most renowned plants that help you sleep. A study by the Miami Miller School of Medicine even showed that the scent of lavender in bath oil calmed babies and sent them off into a deeper sleep. The lavender plant likes warmth and sunlight, so ensure to place it on a sunny windowsill and water sparingly.
